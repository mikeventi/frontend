No Mystery Studio is a Manhattan-based recording studio and multi-format production house that was founded in 1989. Since its inception, No Mystery owner Aleks DeCarvalho has guided the creation of a staggering library of acclaimed musical and cinematic works including contributions to MTV, HBO and network series Point Break, Sex In The City, and numerous Grammy Award winning and multi-platinum artists. In 2007, Aleks partnered with Matthew Kemp, an artist manager and product launch specialist whose client network includes some of the most iconic names in the entertainment industry (Rakim, Alicia Keys, The Kinks, The Marleys, WuTang Clan, lggy Pop, Chrisette Michelle, among many others), to fortify and expand the No Mystery brand beyond the successful studio, and into the broader world of multi-format content production.

While the entertainment marketplace is constantly shifting, the philosophy of “content as the core” consistently stands true. No Mystery Studio embraces this by first creating a superior foundation of product and then customizing it to available outlets internationally, physically and virtually. Strategic alliances (and options to merge or joint venture) with artist labels, advertising agencies, and international distribution outlets.

In 2009, the studio began production of a new film and music project entitled “Two Worlds” a look behind the personalities and processes of writing and recording that occurs when world renowned artists from vastly different musical genres and cultural backgrounds come together. The show will focus on marquee, globally recognized performers such as Rakim, The Marleys, Rammstein’s Richard Kruspe, virtuoso Joshua Bell, Aventura, Kid Rock and KRS-One amongst an ever expanding list in addition to some unknown “Stars of tomorrow” and the celebrities, industry impresarios and entourages that surround them.

Since our inception, we’ve prided ourselves on staying ahead of the technology curve and no where is that more evident than in our state-of-the-art facilities. At the heart is Studio A, which was completely re-imagined in 2011 by renowned studio designer/engineer Richard Oliver (Rolling Stones, Yes) around our brand new SSL AWS 948 Super Analogue Console. The AWS 948 anchors an environment designed to make the fusion of digital and analog audio as seamless as possible, allowing our clients to work more efficiently without compromising sonic quality.

We go to great lengths to foster the creative process of our artists, and make available every amenity to help inspire great work.
